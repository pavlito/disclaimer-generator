2. Customize your Cookie Consent

Users should be able to change their cookies preferences. Include a link/button anywhere on your page so that users can open the Cookies Preferences Center. Example of a link to include on your page that will open the Preferences Center:

<a href="#" id="changePreferences">Change your Cookies Preferences</a>
IDs, classes, tag names can be used as a DOM selector: #changePreferences
#changePreferences
3.Add and organize your JavaScript scripts

This is an optional step, but you can organize your JavaScript scripts directly from this page.

There are four levels of cookie consent available.

4.Copy your Cookie Consent code

cookieconsent.run({"notice_banner_type":"headline","consent_type":"implied","palette":"dark","change_preferences_selector":"#changePreferences","language":"en"});
});
</script>
